Modern life takes place within fully built-out systems of distraction and a loneliness epidemic.

rec·re·a·tion is a nonprofit organization and a social sculpture: an experiment to build scalable structures for individual flourishing. In short, to scale joy.

what
chicago · 2025

now running our first pilot

Weekly gatherings take one of three forms: play (sharing of personal pursuits), pause (reflection), and resolve (intention-setting).

Grounded in Aristotelian friendships of virtue and bell hook's definition of love, our belief is that a sense of both purpose and belonging are critical to thriving.
